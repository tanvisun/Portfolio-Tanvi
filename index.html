About Me

Hi! I'm Tanvi Sawant, a Master's student in Applied Data Science at the University of Southern California, passionate about transforming data into intuitive and impactful visual stories.

With a strong foundation in data visualization tools like Tableau, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly, I specialize in creating clean, insightful dashboards and charts that empower decision-making. My academic and project work spans machine learning, cloud computing (AWS), full-stack development, and MLOps, blending technical depth with a user-first approach.

I bring experience from building real-world systems—like a real-time vehicle detection pipeline, a dental caries detection app, and a crop price forecasting dashboard—all of which integrated modeling, backend services, and compelling visual outputs. Previously, I interned as a Software Developer at Be The Bank, leading a team to build a high-speed payment system using Nest.js, PostgreSQL, and CI/CD pipelines.

Whether it's designing interactive interfaces or interpreting complex data patterns, I'm driven to build solutions that are both technically robust and human-centric.
